Coffee Recipe

Description

Indian Iced Coffee is a versatile and delicious drink that can be enjoyed by coffee lovers of all ages. Whether you prefer it sweet or strong, with or without toppings, there's an Indian iced coffee recipe to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee powder
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (or to taste)
  • Ice cubes
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate syrup (optional)

Steps :

  1. Brew the coffee: In a mug, dissolve the instant coffee powder in a small amount of cold water.
  2. Mix the ingredients: Add the cold milk, sugar, and ice to the mug containing the dissolved coffee. Stir well until the sugar dissolves and the coffee is chilled.
  3. Serve: Pour the iced coffee into a glass and top with whipped cream and chocolate syrup, if desired.
