Indian Samosa Recipe

Description

A crispy, triangular pastry filled with a savory mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and onions. Samosas are a popular street food in India and are served hot with Tea

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • For the filling:
    • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
    • 1 cup peas
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for frying

Steps :

  1. Make the dough:
    • Combine flour and salt in a bowl.
    • Gradually add water and oil, kneading until a smooth dough forms.
    • Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling:
    • Heat oil in a pan.
    • Add onions and cook until golden brown.
    • Add mashed potatoes, peas, cumin powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt.
    • Cook until well combined.
  3. Assemble the samosas:
    • Roll out the dough into a thin sheet.
    • Cut the dough into squares.
    • Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each square.
    • Fold the dough over the filling and seal the edges tightly.
    • Use a fork to crimp the edges.
  4. Fry the samosas:
    • Heat oil in a deep pan.
    • Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
  5. >Serve:
    • Serve hot with chutney or tamarind sauce.
