PaniPuri Recipe

Ingredients

  • For the puris:
    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 cup water
    • Oil for frying
  • For the pani:
    • 1 cup water
    • 1 tablespoon tamarind paste
    • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
    • Salt to taste
    • A pinch of black salt (optional)
  • For the filling:
    • 1 boiled potato, mashed
    • 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas
    • 1/4 cup chopped onions
    • 1/4 teaspoon chaat masala
    • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
    • Salt to taste

Steps :

  1. Make the puris:
    • Combine flour and salt in a bowl.
    • Gradually add water and knead until a smooth dough forms.
    • Rest the dough for 30 minutes.
    • Roll out the dough into thin sheets and cut into circles.
    • Fry the circles in hot oil until golden brown and puffed up.
  2. Make the pani:
    • In a bowl, combine water, tamarind paste, chaat masala, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt.
    • Stir well until the spices dissolve.
  3. Prepare the filling:
    • Combine mashed potatoes, chickpeas, onions, chaat masala, cumin powder, and salt in a bowl.
    • Mix well.
  4. Assemble the pani puri:
    • Fill the puri with a spoonful of the filling.
    • Pour the pani over the filling.
    • Enjoy immediately!
